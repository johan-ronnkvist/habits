Key Points: Better Habits is designed with privacy in mind. Your habit data stays on your device and in your personal Google Drive. We don't collect, store, or sell your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

Data You Provide

  • Habit Information: Names, descriptions, and completion status of habits you create
  • Google Account: Email address when you choose to sync with Google Drive

Data We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect personal information beyond what's necessary for app functionality
  • We do not use tracking cookies or analytics
  • We do not collect device information or usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Local Storage: Your habit data is stored locally in your browser
  • Google Drive Sync: When you enable sync, data is stored in your personal Google Drive folder
  • No Analytics: We do not analyze your usage patterns or habits

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage

  • Habit data is stored locally in your browser using IndexedDB
  • Data remains on your device unless you choose to sync with Google Drive

Google Drive Integration

  • When you enable Google Drive sync, your data is stored in your personal Google Drive
  • We use Google's secure OAuth 2.0 for authentication
  • Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS
  • We only access the specific folder we create for Better Habits backups

4. Data Sharing

  • We do not share your data with third parties
  • We do not sell your information
  • Google Drive: Data synced to Google Drive is governed by Google's privacy policy

5. Your Rights and Choices

  • Access: You can view all your habit data within the app
  • Export: You can export your data through Google Drive sync
  • Delete: You can delete individual habits or all data from the app
  • Disconnect: You can disconnect Google Drive sync at any time

6. Data Retention

  • Local data persists until you clear browser data or uninstall the app
  • Google Drive backups remain until you delete them
  • We do not automatically delete your data

7. Children's Privacy

Better Habits is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

8. International Users

Better Habits is a client-side application. Your data processing occurs on your device and, if you choose, in your Google Drive account, which is subject to Google's data handling practices.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by updating the effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
